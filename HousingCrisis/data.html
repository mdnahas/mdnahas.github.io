Austin's Housing Crisis: Data

I am an economist studying the housing crisis in Austin, Texas. Most of my analysis depends on the work of other economists. I have done two studies myself:

  • A comparison of Austin to the 50 largest cities in America
    • High rent causes homelessness
    • High rent causes homelessness to sleep outside
    • High rent is correlated with a high land price
  • A study of the price of land in Austin
    • Small lots are much more expensive per-acre than large lots

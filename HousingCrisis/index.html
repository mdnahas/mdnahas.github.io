Austin's Housing Crisis

Austin is in a housing crisis. I'm an economist who has studied the issue, so I built this little website to inform people about the problem.

City Rent
Austin $973
Dallas $733
Houston $674
San Antonio $600

My measure of the crisis is the cheapest affordable apartment in a city. The above data is from Zillow.com on Sept. 2021. As you can see, Austin's rent is roughly $250 more than Dallas and $300 more than Houston. Austin is in a housing crisis.
